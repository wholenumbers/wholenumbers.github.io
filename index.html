Our vision

Give what you're good at

wholeNumbers was created to address the skills gap between the analytics industry, and the non-profit sector. We aim to provide analytically-minded professionals with a platform to volunteer their services to non-profit organisations in the way that they know best: data analysis

We also aim to address a perception gap that we sometimes see in the analytics community. Our primary belief is that when used appropriately, simple methods like linear regression or vanilla excel functions like vlookup, are more than sufficient to address primary business needs of non-profit organisations.

Instead of replicating the latest shiny tool in the data science toolkit, our mission is therefore centered on providing sustainable and scalable solutions for non-profits.

Volunteer Process

Our Volunteer Process

General steps for volunteers

01: RECRUIT
Selection and screening of applicants

Interested applicants are welcomed to apply for the project(s) that you are interested in.

In your application, you will be asked to share the key motivation for your interest in volunteering. In the event we have more applicants than required, we might conduct further interviews to find the volunteer with the most appropriate skill-set.

02.MEET
Introductory session

Faciliated by wholeNumbers, the volunteer and the non-profit will meet to formalise the arrangement and services required.

The volunteer will also be required to sign a non-disclosure agreement when required to handle personal data, in accordance with the terms of the Personal Data Protection Act.

03.PARTNER
Relationship building between volunteer and non-profit

At wholeNumbers, we are focused on ensuring scalability and sustainbility of the solution.

Rather than a one-off interaction, the intention is therefore for the volunteer to form a long-term partnership with the non-profit organisation.
